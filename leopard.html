SNOW LEOPARD


Snow leopards, elusive and majestic, inhabit the harsh, high-altitude landscapes of Central Asia. Their silvery-gray fur, adorned with rosette- shaped spots, perfectly camouflages them among rocky terrains. These solitary creatures possess powerful legs, aiding in navigating steep, snowy slopes effortlessly. Their long, thick tails provide balance while leaping across rugged landscapes. Snow leopards are agile hunters, preying on blue sheep and ibexes, ensuring their survival in the extreme conditions of their habitat. Sadly, these exquisite felines face threats from habitat loss and poaching, contributing to their vulnerable status. Conservation efforts strive to safeguard these mesmerising creatures and their delicate ecosystem.
